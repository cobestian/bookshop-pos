Features

Everything Your Shop Needs

From cash sales to wholesale invoicing, inventory to detailed reports — all in one system.

💰
Cash Sales
Record sales instantly with Cash, Mobile Money, and Cheque. Print receipts automatically.
  • Multiple payment modes
  • Auto receipt printing
  • Real-time stock deduction
📦
Inventory Management
Track every product in real time. Low stock alerts, cost & wholesale price management.
  • Real-time stock levels
  • Low stock alerts
  • Cost & profit tracking
🧾
Wholesale Invoicing
Generate professional invoices for wholesale customers. Auto-number and track balances.
  • Auto invoice numbering
  • Customer balance tracking
  • Printable invoices
📊
Reports & Analytics
Daily sales, goods received, expense breakdowns and end-of-day balancing — all printable.
  • Daily & wholesale reports
  • Expense pie charts
  • End of day balancing
👥
Multi-User Access
Role-based access for owners, supervisors, and salesmen. Each sees only what they need.
  • 5 access levels
  • Suspend/activate workers
  • Activity tracking
📱
Mobile Money Tracking
Track all MoMo in/out. See your net balance anytime and filter by date range.
  • MoMo in/out tracking
  • Date range filtering
  • Net balance display
